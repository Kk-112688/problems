Circular Dependency Deadlock Demo

What This Demonstrates:

Three bank accounts trying to transfer money to each other simultaneously:

  • Account A → Account B: Transfer $100
  • Account B → Account C: Transfer $150
  • Account C → Account A: Transfer $200

Each transfer needs to lock both accounts (sender and receiver) to prevent race conditions. This creates a circular dependency that leads to deadlock.
